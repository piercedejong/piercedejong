Stroy Time with Rhys

Story Time

This project was a way to connect a baby with his grandparents virtually and provide interaction to keep the baby (Rhys) engaged. My final design was to do a Read-A-Long allowing both Oma and Opa to read to Rhys and play sounds on some of the pages to help keep Rhys engaged.

Original Design Sketches

Breadth

Originally, I went in many directions for this project, thinking of different ways to keep an 8 month old engaged with virtual people. I finally decided on a read-a-long style project.

Depth

The depth for this project was focused more on the layout of the page than anything else and looking at a possible mobile version.
