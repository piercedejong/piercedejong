How Loud Are You?

How to Play
  • Enter the name of everyone that is playing.
  • Select a game mode.
  • Click on "Test Your Voice".
  • The game will prompt you to begin yelling/matching the noise level into the microphone.
  • Each player will have 3 seconds to play before the next player is up.
  • The winner is decided by who best matches the noise level or is the loudest for the longest.
