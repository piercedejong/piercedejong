Story Time

This project's goal was to make a video-conferencing website to connect Rhys (8 months old) with his Oma and Opa that enganges both parties. I created a "read-a-long" stroy time website using my favorite childhood book "Harold and the Purple Crayon." Oma and Opa can change the pages and play different sound effects to help keep Rhys engaged.

Code from other sources: https://github.com/shanet/WebRTC-Example and Tutorials from CPSC 581 Winter 2021

Voice Matcher

This project was making an interface that had audio as an input but no voice recognition. I created an app to see which person in a group was loudest or could match a decibel level. The inspiration for this project came from my younger brother and his friends shrieking and yelling all of the time; I decided to make an app to see which of them was the loudest.

Code from other sources: https://github.com/cwilso/volume-meter and Tutorials from CPSC 581 Winter 2021
