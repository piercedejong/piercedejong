How Loud are You

This project was an experiment on using audio as one of the main ways to interface with an app. The design constrains disallowed any use of voice recognition or software to discern speech paterns. The project I came up with was based off of seeing which kids screaming contests could be "scientifically measured" to determien the winner.

Original Design Sketches

Breadth

Originally I had many design ideas for using audio in the app. From games to voice tuners to my eventuall design "How Loud Are You?".

Depth

After choosing the project, I next drew out possible design choices for the layout, interaction, and design of the project before choosing a selection of the sketches for the final design.
